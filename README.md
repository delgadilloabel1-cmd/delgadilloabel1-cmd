# Hi, I'm Abel 👋

## 👨‍🏫 About Me
I’m a student at the Marcy Lab School, on a mission to become a Software Engineer. My journey into tech has been anything but traditional  and I love it that way.

For the longest time, I thought I’d be a lawyer. I studied Political Science and Finance at Saint Peter’s University, driven by a desire to make a meaningful impact. But after some time in the classroom, I realized that chasing a “dream career” shouldn’t come at the expense of living life. I took a step back, paused school, and dove into the workforce — from retail to bartending, to client-facing and operations roles. Each experience taught me something new, and I came away with a well-rounded perspective on people, business, and how work fits into life.

Eventually, I realized that tech was my next frontier. The field is fast-moving, endlessly innovative, and offers the flexibility and growth I crave — while still leaving space to enjoy life outside of work. I thrive on challenges, love taking risks (especially on myself), and I’m a certified adrenaline junkie, always chasing the next adventure.

When I’m not coding, you can find me watching movies, exploring new tech, hanging out with my dog Canelo, or spending time with friends and family. I’m always excited to connect, collaborate, and learn, so feel free to reach out via email: delgadilloabel1@gmail.com
